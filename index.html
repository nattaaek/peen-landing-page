FAQ

Does this work offline?
Yes. You can pre-download areas and keep logging sessions without signal.
Is my data private?
Yes. Your notes stay on your device and any sync is opt-in. See our Privacy Policy for details.
What platforms do you support?
Peen is available on iOS and Android. A web companion may come later.
How much does it cost?
The app is free to download. If we add paid plans or in-app purchases, we’ll show pricing clearly in the stores and in the app.
